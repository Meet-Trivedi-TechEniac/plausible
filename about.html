Our Story

12 years of
deliberate craft

Founded in 2012, Meridian was born from a simple but radical idea — that strategy and design are not separate disciplines but a single, unified force.

We started as a two-person consultancy in San Francisco. Today we are a 24-person studio with clients across 40 countries, but the founding principles have never changed.

We do not chase volume. We take on a limited number of engagements each quarter so every project receives the full weight of our attention.

2012
Founded in San Francisco
Elena Calder and Marcus Vance start Meridian as a boutique brand consultancy.
2015
First Major Rebrand
Led the complete repositioning of a $500M fintech firm — our first landmark project.
2018
Expanded to Digital
Launched our digital design and growth practice, tripling the team within 18 months.
2021
Global Reach
Crossed 40 countries served. Opened a second studio in London.
2024
240 Projects Delivered
Reached a new milestone with a 98% client satisfaction rate across all engagements.
What We Stand For

Our values are not
a poster on the wall

✦
Intentional Craft
Every pixel, every word, every strategy is deliberate. We reject speed for its own sake and embrace the complexity required to produce work that lasts.
◈
Strategic Depth
We operate where business logic and creative vision intersect. Beautiful work that does not perform is decoration. Strategy without soul is forgettable.
⬡
Radical Partnership
We embed alongside your team. We share accountability for outcomes, not just deliverables. Your success is our only metric.
△
Honest Counsel
We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. Difficult truths delivered early save years of wasted effort.
○
Long-term Thinking
We optimize for enduring brand equity, not vanity metrics. The brands we build are still relevant a decade after we touch them.
□
Continuous Rigor
Markets change. Audiences evolve. We bring relentless curiosity to every engagement, never assuming yesterday's answers work today.
